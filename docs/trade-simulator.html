The Trade Problem

Under COLA, when a team wins a top-4 draft pick, their lottery index is reduced. But what happens when the pick was traded? If the Clippers trade a pick to the Sixers and it becomes #1 overall, whose index should be reduced?

This is one of the key design decisions for any practical COLA implementation. Prof. Highley's analysis identifies four options, each with different trade-offs. This tool lets you see how each option would have played out across 26 seasons of real NBA history.

Across our dataset, 63 lottery picks (1-14) were traded before the draft, including 12 in the top 4 that trigger COLA index diminishment.
